I'm Serpil Ural, a Senior Product Marketer based in Vienna. Over the past decade, I've worked across consumer brands at Reckitt Benckiser and regulated FinTech at Bitstamp (acquired by Robinhood), where I led product marketing for derivatives, yield products, and the consumer app.

I navigate regulated environments, translating complex products into clear, compliant messaging. I've partnered across Legal, Compliance, Product, and Engineering to launch products across European markets, including through MiCA implementation.

Outside of work, I'm building Ural Pistachios, taking a 35-year-old family pistachio farm in southeastern Turkey direct-to-consumer.
